Websites

Educational Excellence Campaign

This website is for the Educational Excellence Campaign that advocates for the raising of money for physics materials at the high school of Newton North. Using bootstrap, w3schools, GSAP, and many other prominent libraries of greatness, this website is both visually and mentally pleasing. We incentivize you to constantly reload the page and admire the beautiful animation. Have a wonderful day and do not forget to donate to the Educational Excellence Campaign!

Sustainability Club

The Newton North Sustainability Club is another group of wonderful humans that received a marvelous website from us. We strategically and mathematically calculated the rgb values of the colors on this site to complement the authentic design made by the sustainable dev. team. With forms that actually work and padding that pleases the eye, a smile is guaranteed to come to your face when you visit this website. Enjoy and be sustainable!

French Club

The French Club is another club at North that the Programming Club made a site for! We used Bootstrap and brilliant thinking to artistically craft a pleasing and visually appealing site that greatly benefits the French Club. Au revoir!

Photography Club

The cinematic and innovative design applied to this high-end website was created after long nights of debugging and frustration. Intentionally articulated to display the first-rate creation of our dear partners, the Newton North High School Photography Club, this site provides our audience with a memorable experience, which is once in a lifetime. Using bootstrap and hard work, the Programming Club put together another beautiful website. We hope you thoroughly enjoy!

Old Club Website

After the pandemic, the club decided it wanted to entirely redo the website (taking ideas from the old website). We completely redid it trying to add the most useful information possible while still making it cool and modern. Then, we had another epiphany and decided to redo everything once again. As time evolves, so does the Programming Club. Darwin was right all along.

Tiger Insider

The Tiger Insider website uses the bootstrap CSS framework and silverstripe PHP for the backend. The club created some graphics to slap on the website and hired a gfx artist online to create an article placeholder banner. We like to use our own code, so bootstrap CSS was the optimal way in efficiency and creativity.

Dreamher Charity

The website made for Dreamher Charity was also created with bootstrap to create a better user-interface. This site was created for an organization. We hope to continue helping out charities near us by creating websites and games.
