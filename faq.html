FAQ

We've got answers for your burning questions.

When do we meet?

Mondays after school in room 464!

What if I already know how to code?

That’s great! We'd still love to have you in the club. The club is also a space for you to help others and work on your personal projects.

Do I need any experience?

No! Everybody is welcome to join! There is no experience needed!

What are our goals for the year?

  1. Have fun
  2. Make cool projects
  3. Create safe community
  4. Eat good food

Who is the advisor?

Mr.Peloquin (He teaches math at North).

What does the club do?

You meet other coders, make apps, make games, make websites, attend workshops, and play trivia. Most importantly, we eat lots of snacks, dressed in our cool merch!
